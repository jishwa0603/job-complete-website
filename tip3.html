Dress Professionally

Tip Overview

Make sure to dress professionally according to the company culture.

Key Points

  • Understand the company's dress code.
  • Choose conservative colors like black or navy.
  • Ensure your clothes are clean and wrinkle-free.
  • Avoid overly casual clothing.
  • Wear closed-toe shoes for a polished look.
  • Accessorize minimally.
  • Groom yourself well (hair, nails, etc.).
  • Dress one level above the expected dress code.
  • Prepare your outfit the night before.
  • Be comfortable in what you wear.

Manage Stress

Tip Overview

Learn techniques to manage stress and stay calm during interviews.

Key Points

  • Practice deep breathing before the interview.
  • Visualize a positive outcome.
  • Prepare thoroughly to boost your confidence.
  • Take breaks to recharge your mind.
  • Stay positive and replace negative thoughts.
  • Arrive early to avoid last-minute stress.
  • Have a backup plan for unexpected issues.
  • Limit caffeine intake before the interview.
  • Focus on the present moment, not the outcome.
  • Remember that it's a conversation, not an interrogation.

Ask Questions

Tip Overview

Prepare insightful questions to demonstrate your interest in the role.

Key Points

  • Prepare 3-5 thoughtful questions.
  • Avoid asking about salary or benefits too early.
  • Ask about team dynamics and culture.
  • Inquire about the next steps in the hiring process.
  • Show interest in the company's future plans.
  • Ask about opportunities for growth and development.
  • Relate your questions to the role and responsibilities.
  • Be curious about recent projects or challenges.
  • Avoid asking questions that can be easily found online.

Maintain Eye Contact

Tip Overview

Maintaining eye contact shows confidence and engagement.

Key Points

  • Practice making eye contact during conversations.
  • Avoid staring; balance your gaze.
  • Look at the interviewer while they speak.
  • Use facial expressions to convey interest.
  • Avoid looking at your phone or watch.
  • Focus on the interviewer's eyes.
  • Remember to blink to avoid discomfort.
  • Look around the room occasionally.
  • Use eye contact to build rapport.
  • Be mindful of cultural differences in eye contact.

Prepare for Common Interview Questions

Tip Overview

Familiarize yourself with common interview questions and prepare your responses in advance.

Key Points

  • Practice answers to "Tell me about yourself".
  • Prepare for situational questions.
  • Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
  • Be concise in your responses.
  • Relate answers to the job description.
  • Showcase your accomplishments.
  • Rehearse with a friend or mentor.
  • Stay calm and take your time to answer.
  • Listen carefully to the questions.
  • Be honest about your experiences.

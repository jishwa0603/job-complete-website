Follow Up

Tip Overview

Following up after your interview can help reinforce your interest in the position and remind the interviewer of your candidacy.

Key Points

  • Send a thank-you email within 24 hours.
  • Personalize your message by mentioning specifics from the interview.
  • Reiterate your interest in the position.
  • Express appreciation for the opportunity to interview.
  • Keep the email brief and professional.
  • Follow up if you haven't heard back after a week.
